Troubleshooting

  • If camera permission fails, allow camera access and refresh the page.
  • If QR is expired, ask for a newly downloaded credential certificate.
  • If issuer is untrusted, update trusted issuer keys in assets/data/trusted_issuers.json at project root.
  • If your browser blocks scan APIs, try latest Chrome or Edge.

For institutional support, contact your Credify administrator or deployment owner.
